1st Turning Point

In 2012, I was so messed up, even I was not able to solve a single maths problem, 2012 was the first turning point of my life. A year that will change everything for me. In school, I had a fight with my classmate and he challenges me to to compet him in maths. I accepted the challenge but I was so, afraid because, I know I'm so bad at maths. so, here comes the revolution I started solving maths problem and completed my book just in 2 days. I spent my whole two days in solving a book. After two days, here comes the due date, we started our competition at school, and the result were so unbelievable for all. I lost at that challenge, well apparantely everybody knows that I don't have any chances to win. So, the things People are so shocked about, that I gave him very tough competition and at that point, I realise if I'm able to hit him so hard, by just two days of practice. So, what I can when I start practicing regularly and at the end of that year, I represent my School at National Level in maths Olympiad.

So, that was my first turning point in my life, that changes everything. There are many other turning points and interesting Story about me. For, that you have to contact me. I guess there are many turning point in someone's Life, but in my opinion the point where you started believing in yourselves was the most important point.
